Know More About Us

RKMPL is the one of the Company in Madhya Pradesh and Uttar Pradesh that has never faced any penalty, nor has any complaint ever been filed against it.

Coal miners typically work in shifts ranging from 8 to 12 hours, depending on project requirements and safety regulations. At RKMPL, we ensure all work schedules comply with government norms and prioritize worker safety and well-being.

Common PPE (Personal Protective Equipment) for mining includes helmets, safety glasses, ear protection, gloves, high-visibility clothing, steel-toed boots, and respiratory protection. RKMPL strictly enforces PPE usage to maintain a safe working environment.

The risks associated with mining include exposure to harmful dust and chemicals, potential accidents and injuries, and environmental impacts such as habitat destruction and water pollution. RKMPL is committed to minimizing these risks through rigorous safety protocols and environmental management practices.
